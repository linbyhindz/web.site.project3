My Motivation to Study

People love to say that learning must come from pure inspiration. Mine comes from a simpler place: I want a good life. Not yachts or diamonds — just stability, independence, and the ability to buy coffee without calculating my monthly budget in my head. Maybe even a job where I solve real problems instead of surviving them. I do not always enjoy studying. But every topic I master moves me closer to a future where I choose my own path — not the one circumstances push me into. So yes, I study to grow. But I also study to live better. And honestly, that is motivation enough.
